## The Revolutionary Bucket System

### Core Concept
A bucket represents a virtual account for money allocation. Unlike traditional budgeting, buckets create a complete flow system where money automatically distributes according to life priorities and goals.

### Bucket Hierarchy
```
Income Flow
├── Priority 1: Pay Yourself First (5%)
│   └── Long-term Financial Freedom
├── Priority 2: Essential Expenses
│   ├── Rent/Mortgage
│   ├── Utilities
│   ├── Insurance
│   └── Debt Payments
├── Priority 3: Life Necessities
│   ├── Food
│   ├── Transportation
│   └── Healthcare
├── Priority 4: Savings & Protection (30%)
│   ├── Emergency Fund (3-6 months)
│   ├── Medical/Dental Reserve
│   ├── Equipment Replacement
│   ├── Car Maintenance
│   └── Annual Expenses
├── Priority 5: Growth & Development
│   ├── Education/Courses
│   ├── Business Investment
│   └── Skills Development
├── Priority 6: Life Enhancement
│   ├── Play/Entertainment (Must spend monthly!)
│   ├── Social/Relationships
│   └── Hobbies
└── Priority 7: Dreams & Goals
    ├── Travel Fund
    ├── Major Purchases
    └── Long-term Projects
```

### Bucket Mechanics

#### 1. Distribution Rules
- **Equal Distribution**: Default splits evenly among child buckets
- **Weighted Distribution**: Custom percentages per bucket
- **Priority Distribution**: Fill buckets in order until targets met
- **Conditional Distribution**: If-then rules for smart routing

#### 2. Bucket Types
- **Flow-through**: Monthly budgets that reset (food, entertainment)
- **Accumulation**: Savings that build over time
- **Target-based**: Fill to specific amount then stop
- **Perpetual**: Always accepting funds (investments)
- **Protected**: Cannot be withdrawn except emergencies

#### 3. Smart Rules Engine
- "If Emergency Fund < R30,000, redirect 50% of entertainment"
- "When Debt = 0, increase investment bucket by 20%"
- "If month surplus > R5,000, distribute to dreams buckets"

#### 4. Withdrawal Protection Levels
1. **Locked**: Cannot touch (retirement, emergency fund base)
2. **Protected**: Only for designated purpose
3. **Restricted**: Requires cooling-off period
4. **Warning**: Alerts when accessing
5. **Free**: Available for use
